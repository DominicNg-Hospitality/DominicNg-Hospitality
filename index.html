Professional Bio

▼

Dominic Ng is a second-year BSc Management and Information Technology student at Lancaster University, predicted a 2:1, with a strong passion for hospitality and delivering outstanding guest experiences. With over four years of customer-facing experience across bars, cafes, and university venues, he has developed a sharp ability to connect with guests, handle pressure with a smile, and work seamlessly within diverse teams.

Dominic currently works as a Food & Beverage Assistant at Lancaster Marketplace and Lancaster Barkers, where he serves customers, prepares food and beverages, manages cash and card transactions, and maintains a clean and welcoming environment. Previously, he worked as a bartender at The Joyinn in Belfast, where he honed his multitasking, communication, and customer service skills in a high-volume setting.

His hospitality experience is complemented by strong organisational and leadership abilities developed during his time as a Student Prefect and Head Ambassador at Campbell College, where he supported events, represented the school, and coordinated with staff and students.

Why Hospitality? I genuinely enjoy making people feel welcome. Whether it's remembering a regular guest's coffee order, handling a busy Friday night bar shift with a smile, or solving a guest's problem quickly and calmly – I find it rewarding to create positive experiences for others. For me, hospitality is about turning a simple stay or meal into a memorable moment.

Dominic is now seeking a summer seasonal role in hospitality where he can bring his energy, guest-first mindset, and proven teamwork to create memorable experiences for every visitor.
